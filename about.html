About Jenny Linsky

Jenny Linsky is the author of You've Got Kudos, a queer hockey romance novel to be released by Bantam Dell in 2027. She is one of the most popular Hockey RPF writers on Archive of Our Own.

In real life, she lives in the Boston area with her husband and one-year-old daughter and works in Business Intelligence at Harvard, her alma mater. When she's not writing, working, or child-wrangling, she runs a living-room-theater troupe and records a novel-analysis podcast with her husband.

Like so many in fandom, she is queer herself, and she glories in writing agonizing and ultimately joyous stories of queer self-discovery and love.
